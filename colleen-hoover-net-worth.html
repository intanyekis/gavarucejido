What Is The Net Worth Of Writer Colleen Hoover?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 26, 2024

Colleen Hoover net worth currently stands at around $6 million. Colleen earns a good amount of money from her successful writing career.

Hoover's popularity has been increasing since her best-selling romance novel "It Ends With Us" was published. Now the book has its own series and is loved by many, especially young girls.

After graduating high school, she went to Texas A&M University and graduated with a degree in Social Work. Before starting her career as an author, she used to work as a teacher and in the social work field.

She is married to Heath Hoover, and they share three sons together.

Colleen Hoover Net Worth

Colleen Hoover has a staggering net worth of around $6 million.

Colleen wrote her first debut novel "Slammed" back in 2011. She was a self-publishing author in the beginning, and she did it in 2012.

The idea of writing Slammed came to her through one of the Avett Brother's songs, "Head Full of Doubt". She made its lyrics the novel themel and incorporated the idea through her story. 

Her fortune and popularity drastically increased due to her famous romance novel "Its Ends With Us." 

She published the book because she wanted her mother, who had just got Amazon Kindle, to read it. The book did not get much popularity at that time, however, around the beginning of 2012, she published the sequel of the book, "Point of Retreat". 

After a few months, the books became popular after the famous blogger Maryse gave them a 5 stars rating. 

The 5 stars rating skyrocketed her book sales, and she came on the New York Times Bestsellers list in the same year. The books were officially re-published by Atria Publishing Books.

"The Girl" was the third part of the book series, and after their popularity, she became a full-time author. 

She self-published a new novel called "Hopeless" in December 2012, and the book came at the #1 list of the New York Times Best Seller. The book was the first self-published novel ever on the number 1 list.

The companion novel of the book, "Losing Hope" was released in July 2013.

In 2014, she again released a stand-alone novel called Finding Cinderella, and later on, it was rereleased with an updated epilogue.

In the same year, she released Maybe Someday, a romance novel. The book even had an accompanying soundtrack created by the musician Griffin Peterson. 

Her novel "It Ends With Us" was written in 2016 and was the same book that took her to the peak of success.

The book became so famous that it was talked about all over social media and sold over million copies globally. The book has its series, and it turned Colleen into a millionaire.

Colleen Hoover Is The Author Of "It Ends With Us"

Colleen Hoover's It Ends With Us is one of the best-selling romance novels, and the book increased her popularity worldwide. 

She published the book, It Ends With Us back in 2016 and it was one of the most difficult books for her to write.

Atria Publication published the book, and she described the book as "the hardest book I have ever written." She wrote the book based on the relationship between her father and mother.

The novel focuses on domestic violence, and she wrote it to advocate for domestic violence victims. Her childhood experience of growing up in an abusive household has been carried throughout the book.

The book's main plot focuses on the relationship between the three protagonists, Lily, Atlas, and Ryle. 

Lily is a Boston college graduate who has a floral business. One day she develops feelings for the surgeon, Ryle, but he is reluctant to be committed to her. As their relationship slowly goes forward, Lily encounters her first love, Atlas, and their meeting threatens her relationship with Ryle.

Later, Lily is faced with assault and has to make a harsh decision. 

It Ends With Us became popular and sold millions of copies worldwide in 2019.

In the same year, she made it possible for the book to be translated into 20 languages. In 2016, the book won the Goodreads Choice Award for the Best Romance Category. 

Colleen and her book got immensely popular in 2021, especially due to the surge of the #BookTook community on TikTok.

Many book bloggers suggested the book and received lots of love and criticism. In early 2022, It Ends With Us became #1 on the New York Times Bestseller List. 

For the overall 6 months of 2022, the book was on #1 of Publishers Weekley and sold almost 1 million units within a few months. 

It Ends With Us became so famous that it was presented even on billboards around many countries. The book made Colleen attract lots of fans, and she was a part of many book signing events. Still, the novel is kept in the trending sections of many libraries worldwide.
