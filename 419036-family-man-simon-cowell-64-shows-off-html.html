Family Man Simon Cowell, 64, Shows off Rare Photo with Stepson &amp; Son: 'Gorgeous Brothers'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 12, 2024

"America's Got Talent" creator Simon Cowell delighted fans with a rare and affectionate photo of his blended family, introducing "Pebbles," their new dog. He emphasized that family has become his priority after years in the limelight.

Simon Cowell during the quarterfinals episode of "America's Got Talent" in 2022 | Source: Getty Images

Talent show judge Simon Cowell, 64, took to Instagram to share a rare family photo. The snapshot shows him with his fiancée, Lauren Silverman, son, Eric, and stepson, Adam, each holding a cute dog, against the backdrop of a serene garden.

The caption reads, "A couple more of us and Pebbles," indicating the new addition to their family. This post quickly garnered attention, with fans pouring their affection and observations.

Followers expressed their emotions with comments like "true love," capturing the essence of the family's bond. On seeing Eric and Adam together, a fan commented, "gorgeous brothers."

Another fan penned, "such a beautiful family," a sentiment echoed across the comment section. The familial likeness didn't go unnoticed, with a user pointing out how Eric is the "double" of his father.

Just before unveiling the full family portrait, Simon Cowell introduced fans to the newest addition to their family with a pair of adorable photos, captioned, "Our family has had a new arrival... her name is Pebbles." The pictures show the music mogul with the fluffy new family member.

Cowell, known for his sharp wit on "America's Got Talent," has increasingly shown his softer side, emphasizing the importance of family. His engagement, after almost a decade with Silverman, was a family affair, which took place in January 2022.
