Ken Gaulden Biography

Ken Gaulden is the younger brother of the famous American rapper, Kentrell Gaulden, who is better known by his stage name, NBA YoungBoy. NBA Ken has appeared alongside his brother on ‘Star Instagram Live’ on a few occasions.

He also has his own Instagram handle, which has more than 180,000 followers. Though he is mainly known as the brother of one of the most talented young rappers, he has got his own fan following. Whenever he goes live on social media, he gets a massive response from the audience. In fact, his live sessions are often joined by internet celebrities like CJ So Cool. NBA Ken was also featured in one of the song videos of his brother. In the video titled Wat Chu Gone Do, NBA Ken was cast as the younger version of NBA YoungBoy.

Ken Gaulden Career

NBA Ken started appearing alongside his brother on ‘Star Instagram Live,’ which is a live chat session that allows celebrities to interact with their fans and followers. Apart from featuring Ken, NBA YoungBoy has also featured his kids and some of his other family members on ‘Star Instagram Live.’

After appearing live on a couple of occasions, NBA Ken came up with his own Instagram page. Since he was already known by many as NBA YoungBoy’s little brother, his Instagram page started gathering followers almost instantly.

Soon, NBA Ken had his own fan base as people started following him for his innocence and humility. With more than 180,000 followers on Instagram, NBA Ken now gets a massive response from the audience whenever he goes live on ‘Star Instagram.’

NBA Ken’s popularity increased when his brother featured him in one of his song videos. Titled as YoungBoy Never Broke Again – Wat Chu Gone Do ft. Peewee Longway, the song video portrays NBA Ken as the younger version of his brother NBA YoungBoy.

The song video of Wat Chu Gone Do went on to gather more than 14 million views on YouTube, making NBA Ken popular in the rap scene. Also, Ken was appreciated by many for displaying some decent acting skills in the video.
