Adam DeVines Bulge: Workaholics Star Reveals Huge Package On Instagram

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 17, 2024

Adam DeVine is having a GREAT time filming in Hawaii, but things appeared to get a BIT uncomfortable when he set out on a zip lining adventure on July 12. The 'Workaholics' star uploaded a pic to Instagram from his fun day, but all eyes are on the GIANT bulge sticking out of his shorts. Hot damn! No shame here! Adam DeVine, 31, left little to the imagination while zip lining on July 12, as the contraption's harness rode up his crotch and revealed his massive package. But the actor wasn't ashamed of the photo, and even shared it on his Instagram page with a hilarious caption. See it here!

“Zip lining is so fun and not uncomfortable in any way,” Adam sarcastically wrote beside the pic. “#battleofthebulge #MikeandDaveNeedWeddingDates.” The final hashtag is, of course, referring to the movie he’s currently filming in Hawaii with Zac Efron.

The movie also stars Anna Kendrick and Aubrey Plaza, and is set to hit theaters in July 2016. In the film, Adam and Zac star as brothers who buy personal ads to find dates to a wedding, and by the looks of how much fun the cast and crew are having on-location, this movie will probably be pretty amazing!

And, don’t worry, a little discomfort probably won’t deter Adam from zip lining ever again! He also shared a video from the  fun-filled day, which he captioned, “When I see a line I GOTS to zip it! Thanks Kualoa Ranch for the super fun times at your next level beautiful location.” Hey, when you get to spend several weeks in Hawaii, you can’t really complain, right!?

The group has also gone four-wheeling, waterfall jumping, golfing and swimming with tiger sharks throughout their trip, and Adam has been documenting the entire thing on his Instagram account. Seriously, HOW can we get in on all this fun?! Zac being shirtless a majority of the time doesn’t hurt, either!
