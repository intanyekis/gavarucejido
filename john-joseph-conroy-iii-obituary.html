John Joseph Conroy III Obituary

John Joseph Conroy III, a beloved individual, passed away on December 28, 2023, at the age of 74. Born on July 30, 1949, in White Plains, New York, he was the son of the late John Joseph Conroy Jr. and Ruth Orce Conroy. John served his country with honor as a member of the United States Army during the Vietnam War.

Early Life and Military Service

John’s early life was marked by his strong sense of duty and patriotism. Growing up in White Plains, New York, he developed a deep love for his country and a desire to serve. This led him to enlist in the United States Army, where he bravely served during the Vietnam War. His dedication and commitment to his fellow soldiers and his country will always be remembered.

Career with the US Postal Service

After his military service, John embarked on a successful career with the US Postal Service. Starting as a mail carrier in White Plains, New York, he quickly rose through the ranks and became a supervisor. His hard work and dedication earned him transfers to Laurinburg, North Carolina, and Pinehurst, North Carolina, where he continued to excel in his role. Eventually, he was promoted to the position of postmaster in Vass, North Carolina, where he served until his retirement after a remarkable 39-year career.

Personal Life and Hobbies

Beyond his professional achievements, John had a rich personal life. He was an avid golfer and found great joy in spending time on the golf course. His passion for the sport was matched only by his love for his cherished Corvette. John’s enthusiasm for life and his hobbies brought him immense happiness and allowed him to create lasting memories with his loved ones.

Surviving Family Members

John is survived by his loving wife of 32 years, Elaine Conroy, who stood by his side through thick and thin. He is also survived by his stepdaughters, Jennifer Kaplan, Lisa Melillo, and Lori DeCelie, who brought immense joy to his life. Additionally, he leaves behind his sisters, Ruth Ann Conroy of Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, and Sharon Donohue of Spring Lake, New Jersey. John’s legacy lives on through his eight grandchildren, six great-grandchildren, niece Hailey Donohue, and numerous other beloved family members and friends.

Celebration of Life
