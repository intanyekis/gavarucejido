Singer’s Bio

Artist Julius Dubose, also known as A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie, was born on December 6, 1995, in The Bronx, New York City. Dubose was born in the Highbridge neighborhood of the Bronx, and his parents are both from Jamaica. He grew up listening to hip-hop legends such as Jay-Z and 50 Cent and started rapping by the time he was a teenager.

He followed up his debut album with “Hoodie SZN,” which was released in 2018. The album featured collaborations with artists such as Juice WRLD, 6ix9ine, and Offset. “Hoodie SZN” debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart and was certified Platinum.
