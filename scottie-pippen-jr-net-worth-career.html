Scottie Pippen Jr. Carrying His Father's Legacy! Know About His Career and Net Worth Thecelebscloset

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024

The great use of life is to spend it for something that will outlast it, and Scottie Pippen just did that to embark his family legacy to his offsprings. Taking the big responsibility is his son, Scottie Pippen Jr., who, fortunately, chose a basketball career itself, the sport that made his dad a legend.

As the son of six-time NBA winner Scottie, one might say Pippen Jr. has enormous shoes to fulfill, but he is on the right path at least. At the mere age of 19, he is a two-time state champion at Sierra Canyon in the high-school league. So, today we bring you all the life details, net worth, and career of the promising Scottie Pippen Jr.

Scottie Pippen Jr. is a Two-Time Winner 

Scottie Pippen’s son, Scottie Pippen Jr., is a two-time champion for Sierra Canyon at the mere age of 19. He is a 3-star recruit who currently plays for Vanderbilt University as a freshman. The basketball player played as the points guard and started on 20 out of 21 games of the 2019-20 season.

Pippen Jr. interestingly received the accolade of SEC Freshman of the Week on November 25, 2019, as he averaged 17 points and 5.5 assists in the particular week. Earlier in the high-school level, he bagged the title of MVP of the Les Schwab Invitational in Hillsboro, Oregon. 

Therefore, as the famous saying, ‘morning shows the day,’ its safe to say that Pippen Jr. will probably be on the NBA very soon. 

Scottie Pippen Jr. Belongs to A Star-Studded Family

Everyone knows the star, Scottie Pippen Jr,’s dad, Scottie Pippen, from his 17-season long NBA career. However, that doesn’t limit the celebrity name in the Pippen bloodline. His mom. Larsa Pippen, although divorced with Scottie, featured in The Real Housewives of Miami in 2011.

On the other hand, his sister, Sophia Pippen, rose to fame after competing on the reality show Dancing with the Stars. She, alongside partner Jake Monreal, impressed many with their performances and got into the top ten of the contest.

YouTube: Scottie Pippen Jr.’s impressive performance vs Austin Peay.

Pippen’s younger brothers, Justin Pippen and Preston Pippen, also play basketball in the high-school levels. Preston and Sophia both starred in the 2017 online diversity campaign, Faces of the World.

How Much is Scottie Pippen Jr.’s Net Worth?

Scottie Pippen Jr. owns a six-figured fortune figure. Specifically, he accumulates a staggering net worth of $500,000 at the mere age of 19. The primary source of his career undoubtedly is his freshman basketball career. 

Meanwhile, his dad, Scottie Pippen, is a multi-millionaire with $50 million net worth. Likewise, he listed their 2.6 acre Illinois mansion for $2.25 million back in 2019. Thus, with such a significant personal income and a multi-millionaire dad, Pippen enjoys a lavishing life with his family.

Quick Facts: Scotty Pippen Jr.

  • The global renown brand, Nike, selected him to compete in the 2017 Nike Elite 100.
  • Pippen was one of the top scorers at Pine Crest School in Fort Lauderdale in his early days.
  • His dad, Scottie Pippen, used to be a longtime teammate of basketball legend Michael Jordan.
  • He joined Vanderbilt University from Sierra High School in January 2019.
  • The basketball star is quite active on Instagram, with about 190,000 followers.
