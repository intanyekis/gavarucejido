Jax Cinderella Snapped Lyrics: A Feminist Anthem for the Modern Princess

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 9, 2024

Who is Jax and what is Cinderella Snapped?

Jax is an American singer-songwriter who rose to fame after competing in the fourteenth season of American Idol, where she finished in third place. She is known for her distinctive voice, quirky style, and original songs that often challenge the status quo.

One of her most popular songs is Cinderella Snapped, which was released in March 2023. The song is a catchy and witty twist on the classic fairy tale of Cinderella, where the princess decides to take matters into her own hands and stand up for herself and other women. According to Genius1, Jax said that she wanted to write a song from Cinderella’s perspective, if she and all the princesses rewrote their stories and burned the fairytale system down.

What are the lyrics of Cinderella Snapped?

The lyrics of Cinderella Snapped are a clever mix of humor, sarcasm, and empowerment. The song starts with the familiar story of Cinderella waiting for her prince to return her glass slipper, but then reveals that he was cheating on her with her stepsister. Cinderella then decides to snap and burn his castle down, while singing:

I don’t need no prince to save me I’m a goddamn CEO Don’t call me “Baby”, equal pay me Snow White said you tried to kiss her So I’ll just buy a new glass slipper And burn your castle down And kids, that’s how Cinderella snapped

The song then goes on to describe how other Disney princesses also rebelled against their stereotypical roles and achieved their own goals. For example, Rapunzel shaved her head, Jasmine made out with Mulan, Sleeping Beauty sued the guy who kissed her while she was asleep, Ariel was confident without any feet, Tiana got a biomedical degree, and Beauty realized that she was the Beast.

The song ends with a powerful bridge where Cinderella confronts the prince and accuses him of treason for cheating on her. She also mocks him for calling her emotional and controllable, while she studied his own legislation and used it against him.

Why is Cinderella Snapped a feminist anthem?

Cinderella Snapped is a feminist anthem because it challenges the traditional and patriarchal narratives of fairy tales that often portray women as passive, dependent, and submissive. The song empowers women to be independent, assertive, and ambitious, and to pursue their own dreams and happiness. The song also celebrates diversity and inclusion by featuring different princesses from different cultures, races, and sexual orientations.

The song has resonated with many listeners who relate to its message of female empowerment and liberation. The song has also become a viral sensation on TikTok2, where users have created videos using the song as a soundtrack for various scenarios where they snap or stand up for themselves.

Conclusion

Jax Cinderella Snapped Lyrics are a refreshing and fun take on the classic fairy tale of Cinderella. The song is a feminist anthem that encourages women to break free from the oppressive and outdated norms of society and to embrace their own identities and potentials. The song is also a catchy and witty tune that showcases Jax’s talent and creativity as a singer-songwriter.
