Anna Konkle Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Who is Anna Konkle?

Anna Konkle is an American actress, director, and producer. Anna is famous for RoseWood TV Series (2015) in the role of Tara Milly Izikoff, Plus One comedy-drama (2019) as a Jenna, and PEN15 TV Series(2019) in the role of Anna Kone.

Anna Konkle: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Anna was born on 7th April 1987 in Randolph, Vermont, U.S. She is the daughter of James Konkle and Janet Ryan. However, in 1994 her family moved to Scituate, Massachusetts. Talking about her nationality, she holds an American Identity with mixed( German, English, Irish) ethnicity.

Konkle studied at the public schools of Scituate, Massachusetts. Anna enrolled at New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts. As of 2022, she is 35 years old.

Anna Konkle: Career

Actor

Anna debuted in the Last Call’s short film (2012) in the role of Kristen. In the following year, she played the role of Enid in Betas (TV Series).

Similarly, Konkle was seen as a Judy in New Girl (TV Series) 2014. In 2015, she was seen in many TV Series like Self Conscious Workout, Man Seeking Woman, Big Time in Hollywood, FL, and The Escort.

Moreover, in 2017  she made her appearance in the Men short film in the role of Amber. In the same year, Anna played the Shameless (TV Series) as a Tenant.

Konkle made her appearance as a guest in many TV Series(2019) such as Conan, WTF with Marc Maron, and the 2019 Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Awards as well.

Furthermore, in 2020 Konkle played the role of IZZY in Big Mouth( TV Series). In 2021, she was seen as a Shayleen in Together Together(comedy-drama). In the same year, she appears in PEN15 (TV Series) in the role of Anna Kone.

Producer, director

Anna was the producer of Project Reality (a  TV Mini-Series) in 2013. From 2019 to 2019, she produced many Showrunners such as Yuki, Grammy, Luminaria, Runaway, and Home.

Besides that, Konkle writes Three TV Series. In the same year, she wrote Vendy Wiccany Tv Series. Anna writes Jacuzzi TV Serie in 2021. She writes Yuki Comedy as well.

Anna Konkle: Awards and Nominations

Anna has won Gotham Independent Film Award for PEN15 (2019). She is the nominee for INOCA TV. Similarly, she is nominated for WGA Award.
