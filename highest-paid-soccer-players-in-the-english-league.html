The Highest-Paid Football Players In The English League

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 17, 2024

Highlights

  • The English Premier League is highly competitive and attracts talented players with its high salaries and glamorous lifestyle.
  • Manchester City is a top club in the EPL, signing players like Bernardo Silva, Jack Grealish, Marcus Rashford, Raheem Sterling, and Kevin De Bruyne.
  • These players, along with others like Raphaël Varane and Erling Haaland, command substantial annual salaries, making them some of the highest-paid football players in the league.

The most popular sport in the world is unarguably football (known as soccer in the United States); the English Premier League is the top tier of league soccer, and as such, it attracts a lot of money, glamour, criticism, and dedication to the craft.

The English Premier League, also known as EPL, is considered by some to be the most competitive league. At the same time, the EPL has witnessed a series of talented players grace the stadiums.

The English Premier League ranks as the highest paying soccer league, with approximately 4.7 billion fans. Professional football has always been known for its massive weekly paycheck – and its players are famous for their luxurious lifestyle, from pricey cars to premium mansions (and various business investments).

10 Bernardo Silva

Football Club:

Manchester City

Annual Salary:

$16.5 Million

Bernardo Silva is a talented Portuguese soccer player, born on August 10th 1994. He represents his home country, Portugal, and plays as an attacking midfield or right-winger for the English Premier League club Manchester City.

Bernardo Silva is known for his creativity in the midfield, close control, skills, and versatility, making him one of the best midfielders in the world. From Benfica, Silva made his debut in 2013.

His impressive performance led him to the second club he played for (AS Monaco) in 2014. His rise to prominence did not show signs of slowing down – and then he attracted the eyes of English giants.

Manchester City signed Bernardo Silva in 2017, and offered him an annual salary of $16.5 million. Bernardo Silva is still committed to improving his craft as his future remains bright.

9 Jack Grealish

Football Club:

Manchester City

Annual Salary:

$16.5 Million

Like many British footballers, Jack Grealish began his illustrious career in the English Premier League. He played in the youth ranks of Aston Villa as an attacking midfielder or left-winger.

In 2014, he made his debut for the senior team; Jack Grealish is famous for his quick feet, agility, close control, passes, and dribbles. He played a critical role in assisting Aston Villa to secure a spot in the FA Cup finals.

In 2020, he made his international debut for England, and has grown from strength to strength. Jack Grealish's creativity and ability to take on defenders make him an asset to any team, and put him on the radar of many big clubs.

In 2023, Manchester City secured Jack Grealish for a British record fee of $105.9 million on a six-year deal and an annual salary of $16.5 million, putting him on the same level as Portuguese teammate Bernardo Silva.

8 Marcus Rashford

Football Club:

Manchester United

Annual Salary:

$16.5 Million

Marcus Rashford is a youthful talent, born on October 31st 1997. He is a product of the Manchester United youth team system, and plays for the England national team.

Rashford is capable of playing as a forward, winger, or attacking midfielder, and he took the crowd by surprise by scoring two goals on his first team and European debut; he also went on to score on his EFL Cup and UEFA Champions League debut. He is much-publicized for his agility, speed, and goal-scoring ability.

Marcus Rashford is a talented soccer star, philanthropist, and social activist, and it's no wonder he ranks high on this list. On July 19th 2023, Manchester United extended his contract, locking him down through 2028 with a whopping $16.5 million annual salary – level with Jack Grealish and Bernardo Silva.

7 Raheem Sterling

Football Club:

Chelsea

Annual Salary:

$17.9 Million

This Jamaican-born star moved to England at the age of five and started his soccer career with Queens Parks Rangers FC (QPR). In 2010, he joined Liverpool and debuted for the senior team in 2012.

He formed a deadly attacking combination with Luis Suárez and Daniel Sturridge during his time with Liverpool. He is well known for his exceptional dribbles and versatility on the field, which brought the interest of some clubs.

Eventually, Manchester City was able to sign Sterling, where he kept on improving and played a vital role in the success of Manchester City over the years. Finally, in 2022, Sterling signed a five-year contract with Chelsea FC, making him the sixth highest-paid player in English Soccer, with an annual salary of $17.9 million.

6 Raphaël Varane

Football Club:

Manchester United

Annual Salary:

$18.7 Million

Raphaël Varane is one of the best center-backs in the world; he is well-known for his defensive ability and pace. Varane currently represents the English Premier League side Manchester United, and France internationally.

Raphaël Varane started his youth career with AS Hellemmes and joined Lens shortly after. His talent was evident, which drew the interest of many clubs; he signed with the Spanish giants Real Madrid in 2010.

His journey with the Spanish giants was fruitful, as they won 18 trophies in ten seasons, including three La Liga and four Champions Leagues (among other awards and titles). In addition, Varane played an instrumental role in helping France secure their second World Cup title.

In 2021, Manchester United secured the services of Varane, who received an annual salary of £17.7 million. Varane's professionalism and quiet leadership have strengthened Manchester United's defense and given it more depth.

5 Jadon Sancho

Football Club:

Manchester United

Annual Salary:

$19.2 Million

This English forward started his career in the youth ranks of the German giant Borussia Dortmund, and made his senior debut in 2017. Jadon Sancho was one of the most outstanding young players in the Bundesliga, and his dynamic style of play made him unique.

He was named runner-up in the Golden Ball and Kopa Trophy awards in 2019. Manchester United came calling in 2021, and Jadon Sancho answered, leading to a contract with an annual salary of $19.2 million.

Sancho has been representing Manchester United at the club level, and England internationally.

4 Mohamed Salah

Football Club:

Liverpool

Annual Salary:

$19.2 Million

Mohamed Salah was born in Nagrig in Egypt and started his career in Egypt, but soon drew interest from different clubs in England; Chelsea was the club to secure his services. Salah played for Chelsea, and represented Egypt internationally.

He struggled in Chelsea, leading the team to loan Salah to Fiorentina and Roma, who eventually poached him from Chelsea. Salah was a crucial part of his Roma team until he signed for Liverpool in 2017 – for a then-record club fee, with an annual salary of $19.2 million.

Salah hit the ground running with a record for the most Premier League goals scored in a 38-game season in 2017 and 2018. Furthermore, he played a vital role in the success of Liverpool since his arrival at Anfield.

3 Casemiro

Football Club:

Manchester United

Annual Salary:

$19.2 Million

Brazilian defensive midfielder Carlos Henrique Casimiro (known mononymously as Casimiro) established his illustrious career in the youth ranks of São Paulo FC shortly after he debuted with the senior team in 2010.

As a young boy, he showed his defensive prowess and proclivities in the midfield, which earned him recognition from Real Madrid. Casemiro is famous for his work rate, physicality, and regaining possession on the pitch.

Casimiro played a vital role in the many successes and trophies enjoyed by Real Madrid up until 2022, when Manchester United signed him to a four-year contract – including an annual salary of $19.2 million, which ties him with Liverpool's Mohamed Salah and Manchester United's Jadon Sancho.

2 Erling Haaland

Football Club:

Manchester City

Annual Salary:

$20.6 Million

This Norwegian machine took the soccer world by storm. Erling Haaland is famous for his pace, strength, and outstanding ability to score goals, and he is considered one of the best players of his generation.

Haaland started his career as an ordinary young athlete in Norway, playing for a series of youth teams. Significant advancement in his career started at Molde FK, where he made his professional debut in 2017.

His supercharged performance drew the attention of Red Bull Salzburg in Austria, and there, he dominated the league with his strength. But it was apparent Haaland wanted more.

The year 2020 saw a high-profile move to Borussia Dortmund, where Haaland continued business as usual, as he scored for fun and dominated. In 2022, he signed a contract for Manchester City which guarantees him a substantial annual salary of $20.6 million.

1 Kevin De Bruyne

Football Club:

Manchester City

Annual Salary:

$22 Million

Kevin De Bruyne is a soccer magician who represents and captains Manchester City at the club level, and the Belgian international team.

De Bruyne began his football journey in Belgium with Gent, and moved to KRC Genk afterward. His performance drew the attention of Chelsea in 2012, who later signed him.

He struggled in Chelsea and later moved to Wolfsburg in the Bundesliga, where he improved excessively, showcased his exceptional vision, versatility, and creative ability, and was soon in the limelight. Manchester City eventually signed him in 2015, with a payment in the vicinity of $22 million yearly.

De Bruyne became the focal point of Manchester City, and is considered one of the best midfielders of his generation. Unsurprisingly, he tops the list of the highest-paid football players in the English Premier League, and proved to be worth every penny.
