Who is Ally Love's husband Andrew Haynes?

Ally married Andrew Haynes in a five-day affair in Mexico in 2021. Andrew, who hails from Trinidad and Tobago, is a corporate financier based in New York. Despite the differences in their upbringing, Andrew and Ally share multiple common interests. They are both passionate about traveling and have visited more than 22 countries together.
