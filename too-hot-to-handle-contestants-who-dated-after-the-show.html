Too Hot To Handle Contestants Who Dated After The Show

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 22, 2024

When Netflix's hottest reality show Too Hot to Handle premiered in April 2020, viewers had to handle the heat, watching sexed-up twenty-somethings with banging bodies and the whole package going on for them, enter the villa as singles, believing they would be having the best mingling summer of their lives in an exotic villa. However, Lana had something else in store for them - A smooch, self-gratification and sex ban!

RELATED: Exclusive: How Too Hot to Handle Shaped Jaz Holloway's Dating Life

After three seasons and Lana's rigorous workshops, meditation and bonding exercises, these horny singles who have had meaningless sex all their lives are transformed into forming meaningful connections, and sometimes finding love that goes beyond the villa. They have also found fame after the show, with massive success and mouth-watering net worths, a decent following on social media, and a whole new life in the public sphere. So here's a look at the Too Hot to Handle alums who dated during the show, and continued their romance outside the villa, as well as some who got together after the show wrapped.

9 Rhonda Paul And Sharron Townsend

The hot mom and model connected with Sharron on deep emotional levels, with the pair already discussing plans outside the villa. Unfortunately, their relationship didn't make it in the real world.

Back in April 2020, Rhonda revealed during interview with Women's Health that the two were no longer together due to the distance, and that they have not even had the time to meet physically since the show. However, they remained friends.

8 Joey Joy And Carla Lawrence

The two began their romance after Carly had broken it off with Chase DeMoor in season 2. This new romance was a breath of fresh air for Carly. They couple briefly broke up after filming wrapped, but rekindled their romance.

After moving in together, and getting matching tattoos, they split for the second time. Carly is now dating model Bennett Sipes, and she shares her man-smitten updates on social media.

7 Christina Carmela And Robert Van Tromp

These two joined the season 2 party mid-way, but made it worth our while. What seemed like more of a physical connection and sexual tension which removed them from the show turned out to be a big deal - at least for a while.

However, the two broke up late last year after celebrating 8 months together in August 2021. Robert spoke about the breakup in September that same year on his Instagram, and told MailOnline that it was a mutual decision.

6 Nicole O'Brien And Bryce Hirschberg

During the show, this pair never dated or gave viewers any incline of a potential romance, just friendship. They got together after season 1 wrapped, and went public with their relationship in April 2020.

Unfortunately, they called it off the following month due to the distance. They even appeared on MTV's Ex on the Beach early this year, with Nicole wanting to get her man back, but the close pair decided to only remain friends.

5 Beaux Raymond And Harry Johnson

Starting off on a non-romantic connection, at least for Harry, the two had one of the sweetest, genuine, and goofiest romance on the show. When they emerged winners of season 3, viewers agreed it was the right choice for this fan-favorite.

The couple posted loved up pictures during the time the show aired in January but they split after returning from the villa. They confirmed their breakup during the show's reunion, but mentioned they still remain friends.

4 Nathan Soan And Holly Scarfone

These two couldn't keep their hands to themselves in season 3, making their time on the villa the most expensive loss by a couple across all seasons, costing their cast mates a whooping $90,000.

The young couple appears to have grown during the show, and even though they weren't crowned winners, their profound growth made their time all worth it. They confirmed their split in January, with the relationship being a struggle due to their workload.

3 Melinda Melrose And Peter Vigilante, Melinda Melrose And Marvin Anthony

Melinda is doing pretty great nowadays hosting her own Netflix show Dated And Related where she turns heads with attention-worthy entrances. But not too long ago, she dated fellow season 2 contestant Peter Vigilante after the show ended. They had many sweet TikTok and Instagram moments, but it appears they may have split, with no posts of them anymore.

On the show, she was with French hulk Marvin Anthony, with the two going on a bumpy ride during the show, but that changed. They grew individually, professed their commitment to each other, with Marvin also winning the show. Unfortunately, they called it quits after the show due to the long distance and series of arguments.

2 Harry Jowsey And Francesca Farago

When the Australian stud set his eyes on the Canadian bombshell in the first episode of season 1, it was butterflies for Harry. As the season progressed, the couple's attraction for each other blossomed into something more than just looks. They began dating on the show, and it seemed things were going to work out for the two notwithstanding the distance. They split, then later reconciled, with the two even getting engaged. Unfortunately, they called it off again.

Harry spoke about the couple having another go at it but it just wasn't working. Fortunately, there seems to be no bad blood between the friendly exes.

1 Cam Holmes And Emily Miller

In something Lana would be proud to see, Cam and Emily are the only couple who are still going on strong after the show. They were contestants on Season 2, and they had their high and low moments on the show, but were able to work out of the villa boyfriend and girlfriend. They also moved in together, and have posted loved-up pictures ever since.

Back in June, Emily revealed that she had an ectopic pregnancy which was a trying time for the two, coming from a breakup rumor which circulated some months before. They may not have won the $100,000 cash prize reward, they have become very successful and won fan's hearts who are constantly rooting for them.
