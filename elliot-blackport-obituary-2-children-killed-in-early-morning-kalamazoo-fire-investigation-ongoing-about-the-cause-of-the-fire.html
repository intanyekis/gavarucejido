Elliot Blackport Obituary, 2 Children Killed In Early-morning Kalamazoo Fire, Investigation Ongoing

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Elliot Blackport Obituary, Death – A fire that broke out in the early hours of Monday morning in Kalamazoo, Michigan, was responsible for the deaths of two young children.

The intersection of East Stockbridge Avenue and Division Street was the location where the event took place, as reported by the Kalamazoo Department of Public Safety (KDPS). KDPS was the source of this information for FOX 17, which was provided to them. It was at around two in the morning when the first calls started coming in. When firefighters arrived, they found a significant amount of smoke in addition to a fire that had swiftly spread across the building’s first and second stories. The fire had been burning for some time before the firefighters arrived.

Reports from KDPS indicate that before firemen arrived on the scene, two people, one adult, and another younger person, were able to successfully flee the building before the firefighters arrived. Since then, the identities of the victims have been revealed to be Evelyn Blackport, who was four years old, and Elliot James Blackport, who was seven years old.

The inquiry into the events that led up to the catastrophe, which is presumed to have been started by mistake, is now still going on. At this time, the Korean Democratic People’s Party (KDPS) would like to express its condolences to the family members of the victims who passed away.
