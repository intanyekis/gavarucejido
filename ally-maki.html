Ally Maki is an American actress and singer who has been working in Hollywood since her mid-teens. She had moved to Los Angeles at the age of 14 to pursue an acting career. Since then, she has landed bit-part roles in popular shows such as The Big Bang Theory, That’s So Raven, and Shake It Up. She got her first big breakthrough in 2016 with an important role in comedy series, Wrecked. She followed it up by bagging an important role in Cloak & Dagger.
