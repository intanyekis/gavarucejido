Is Jonadette Carpio Dating Anyone?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Born in the Philippines, Jonadette Carpio’s interest in dancing developed at an early age as she used to dance in the streets of Manila. After moving to Europe, she was enthralled by the various forms of dance moves of European music videos and films. She decided to learn it and dedicated herself to dancing.

To try out her new dancing skills, she participated in the BBC Young Dancers in 2015. Although she did not win the competition, she won many people’s hearts and gained recognition from the audience. The 23-year-old is not limited to dancing only, as she played a significant part in the recently released movie Cats. If you want to know more about the talented Filipino dancer’s life, then check out the following section!

Who is Former BBC Young Dancers Star Jonadette Carpio in Relationship with? 

Just in her early twenties, the skillful dancer looks to be single right now. There has been no link-ups and relationship rumors about her in social media or news.

The Filipino lady is pretty open about her life and often gives an insight into her daily lifestyle and activities through her social media. There has been, however, no such post where she has indicated that she is in love with anyone.
