These Are The Highest-Grossing Margot Robbie Movies

Highlights

  • Margot Robbie's films have grossed over $3 billion worldwide, solidifying her position as a box office powerhouse.
  • Barbie is Robbie's highest-grossing film, earning over $1.45 billion worldwide and becoming an international sensation.
  • Despite the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, Robbie's films like Peter Rabbit 2 and The Suicide Squad have been successful at the box office.

Over the past decade, Margot Robbie has become one of Hollywood's most popular and bankable stars.

Robbie has demonstrated her adaptability in various genres, from her breakthrough performance in The Wolf Of Wall Street, to her legendary depiction of Harley Quinn.

With her recent performance as Barbie in the critically acclaimed Barbie movie, Robbie's films have grossed over $3 billion worldwide at press time.

Looking at Margot Robbie's highest-grossing films to date, it is evident she is among the highest-paid actresses as well.

Today, she continues attracting large audiences to indie and blockbuster films, with her newest movie, My Old Ass, set to hit cinemas on January 20th, 2024.

10 Peter Rabbit 2: The Runaway (2021)

Total Box Office Gross: $150.9 Million

In the face of the COVID-19 pandemic, Peter Rabbit 2: The Runaway was a remarkable success at the box office in 2021.

Robbie returned to her role voicing Flopsy and the narrator in the live-action/C.G. hybrid sequel, which earned $40 million in the United States and $110 million overseas, bringing its worldwide total to $150.9 million.

With a modest budget of just $45 million, Peter Rabbit 2 defied expectations and emerged as one of the most successful family films of the year.

Against pandemic-related headwinds, the film's performance solidified her position as a box office powerhouse.

Domestic Box Office

$40,501,717

International Box Office

$110,475,047

Worldwide Box Office

$150,976,764

9 The Suicide Squad (2021)

Total Box Office Gross: $167 Million

Margot Robbie reprised her role as Harley Quinn in The Suicide Squad, a 2021 D.C. film directed by James Gunn.

In spite of its release and the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, the R-rated semi-sequel amassed $111 million internationally and $55 million domestically, for a total of $167 million worldwide.

It grossed less than $185 million at the box office and was deemed a financial failure:

Nevertheless, the film's triumphant critical acclaim and Robbie's exemplary portrayal of Harley Quinn's devoted fans deeply moved viewers.

The Suicide Squad demonstrated her star power, irrespective of difficult circumstances.

Domestic Box Office

$55,817,425

International Box Office

$111,280,312

Worldwide Box Office

$167,097,737

8 Focus (2015)

Total Box Office Gross: $168 Million

In the 2015 crime caper, Margot Robbie and Will Smith teamed up as a duo of con artists and former lovers.

With an initial domestic opening of $18 million, Focus accumulated $53 million in North America and an impressive $114 million overseas.

Despite receiving mixed reviews, the film proved to be a solid box office success, grossing $168 million worldwide on a budget of $65 million.

Ranked as one of Robbie's top-performing films, Focus proved to be a lucrative venture for her, as she starred alongside one of Hollywood's most reliable actors at the beginning of her career.

Domestic Box Office

$53,862,963

International Box Office

$114,202,737

Worldwide Box Office

$168,065,700

7 Birds of Prey (2020)

Total Box Office Gross: $201 Million

In the 2020 DC flick Birds Of Prey, Margot Robbie starred as Harley Quinn, the leader of a group of anti-heroines.

Despite positive reviews, its $33 million local opening faltered, because the movie opened in February 2020 – right before the COVID-19 epidemic closed theaters around the world.

Nevertheless, it brought in $84 million in North America and $116 million abroad for a $201 million global total.

Birds Of Prey made a profit on an $82 million budget, once again proving Robbie is a bankable star.

It showcased her range as Harley Quinn, and is among Robbie's highest-grossing movies:

Domestic Box Office

$84,158,461

International Box Office

$116,847,091

Worldwide Box Office

$201,005,552

6 Peter Rabbit (2018)

Total Box Office Gross: $346.7 Million

In February 2018, the live-action/C.G. hybrid film Peter Rabbit debuted, with Margot Robbie lending her voice as the narrator and Flopsy.

The film opened to an impressive $25 million domestically. Thanks to Robbie's involvement, Peter Rabbit reached a total of $115 million in North America.

However, it achieved even greater success internationally, earning a staggering $231 million outside the United States, and ultimately reaching a worldwide total of $346.7 million.

With a modest budget of just $50 million, Peter Rabbit proved a phenomenal success, catapulting it into the ranks of Robbie's top-grossing films.

Its broad appeal to families played a significant role in its triumph.

Domestic Box Office

$115,234,093

International Box Office

$231,493,279

Worldwide Box Office

$346,727,372

5 The Legend of Tarzan (2016)

Total Box Office Gross: $348.9 Million

Margot Robbie and Alexander Skarsgard co-starred in a big-budget rendition of the classic Tarzan narrative in 2016.

Despite mixed reviews, The Legend Of Tarzan grossed $126 million in North America and opened to $38 million domestically.

The picture, however, achieved its true triumph overseas, grossing $222 million for a worldwide total of $348.9 million.

Domestic Box Office

$126,643,061

International Box Office

$222,258,964

Worldwide Box Office

$348,902,025

4 Once Upon A Time... In Hollywood (2019)

Total Box Office Gross: $377.4 Million

Margot Robbie took on the role of Sharon Tate in Quentin Tarantino's 2019 film Once Upon A Time... In Hollywood.

Despite being in a supporting role, Robbie demonstrated her star power by helping draw audiences to the theater. Once Upon A Time... In Hollywood opened to $41 million domestically, foreshadowing its success.

Thanks partly to Robbie's fame, it grossed an impressive $142 million in North America. Overseas, it added another massive $234 million worldwide, a total of $377.4 million.

With Robbie as part of the star-studded ensemble cast, Once Upon A Time... In Hollywood further exemplified her bankability as a leading lady.

Domestic Box Office

$142,502,728

International Box Office

$234,924,175

Worldwide Box Office

$377,426,903

3 The Wolf Of Wall Street (2013)

Total Box Office Gross: $389.8 Million

Margot Robbie rose to prominence when she starred alongside Leonardo DiCaprio in Martin Scorsese's 2013 film The Wolf Of Wall Street.

In what was only her fourth feature film, Robbie made a remarkable impression. The movie was incredibly successful, debuting with an impressive $34 million in domestic box office returns.

The Wolf Of Wall Street earned an impressive $116 million in North America, thanks to strong buzz and positive reviews.

Internationally, it generated an impressive $272 million, bringing its total worldwide earnings to $389.8 million.

Domestic Box Office

$116,949,183

International Box Office

$272,866,953

Worldwide Box Office

$389,816,136

2 Suicide Squad (2016)

Total Box Office Gross: $745.7 Million

Margot Robbie made her debut as Harley Quinn in the DC blockbuster Suicide Squad in 2016, which was one of the first films in the new DCEU.

Robbie's interpretation of the popular character received a lot of attention. The film exceeded expectations by opening to a massive $133 million domestically, breaking records.

It grossed $325 million in North America, due in part to Robbie's breakout performance. Despite negative reviews, it added $420 million to its global total of $745.7 million.

Suicide Squad demonstrated Robbie's ability to gather large crowds early in her career.

As her fame grows, it remains her second highest-grossing film to date.

Domestic Box Office

$325,100,054

International Box Office

$420,644,926

Worldwide Box Office

$745,744,980

1 Barbie (2023)

Total Box Office Gross: $1.45 Billion

Margot Robbie portrayed the main character in Greta Gerwig's 2023 film Barbie:

The film exceeded all expectations, receiving rave reviews and achieving great success at the box office.

Driven by glowing reviews and Robbie's exceptional performance, it made a staggering $636 million in its domestic opening, marking the year's most impressive debut.

Barbie's global earnings have surpassed $1.45 billion and continue to grow.

Robbie's latest film has become an international sensation, raking in an impressive amount of money and surpassing all her previous box-office successes.

$636,233,117

$803,595,496
