Colin Hitschler Wikipedia And Age: Meet Alabama New Assistant Coach

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Colin Hitschler Wikipedia: Alabama plans to hire Wisconsin safeties coach/ co-defensive coordinator Colin Hitschler.

Since he is not considered among the A-listers of the football world, there is currently no Wikipedia page dedicated to him.

With his selection for the new post, fans are curious about his personal life, including his family and background.

Colin Hitschler will soon be the new assistant coach at the University of South Alabama. Currently, he serves as the co-defensive coordinator/safety at Wisconsin.

His coaching journey began as a training camp assistant with the Philadelphia Eagles and as a player personnel assistant with the Kansas City Chiefs.

Additionally, Hitschler has worked at different institutions such as Salve Regina, Widener, and Arkansas State; however, these stints were short-lived.

He then spent time at South Alabama (2014-2017) and Cincinnati (2018-2022), holding various positions, before joining Wisconsin.

Similarly, his brothers, Rob and Colin, remained part of the wrestling coaching at Penn.
